🧁 My Love for Baking

From cookies to cakes — baking is my happy place.

Hi! I'm Ella, and baking has been one of my biggest passions for as long as I can remember. There's something magical about combining simple ingredients and creating something delicious to share with people you love.

⭐ My Favorite Things to Bake

  1. Chocolate Chip Cookies
    • My absolute all-time favorite
    • Tips for the perfect cookie:
      • Brown your butter for extra depth of flavor
      • Chill the dough for at least 30 minutes
      • Sprinkle sea salt on top before baking
  2. Cinnamon Rolls
    • Perfect for weekend mornings
    • Key steps:
      • Don't rush the dough rise
      • Make cream cheese frosting from scratch
  3. Banana Bread
    • Great way to use overripe bananas
    • Add-ins I love:
      • Walnuts
      • Chocolate chips
  4. Layer Cakes
    • My go-to for birthdays and celebrations
    • Favorite combinations:
      • Lemon cake with blueberry buttercream
      • Chocolate cake with salted caramel frosting
  5. Macarons
    • Challenging but so rewarding!
    • Tricks I've learned:
      • Age your egg whites overnight
      • Sift your almond flour twice

💡 My Top Baking Tips

  • Always use room temperature butter and eggs
  • Measure flour by spooning it into the cup, not scooping
  • Read the entire recipe before you start
  • Don't open the oven door too early!
